## Presentation Tips

1. **Pace:** Allow time for questions
2. **Interaction:** Encourage participation
3. **Examples:** Use real-world scenarios
4. **Visuals:** Use screenshots and diagrams
5. **Practice:** Include hands-on exercises
6. **Resources:** Provide takeaway materials
